# Halen — The Finishing-Salt Authority Moat

> Objective: become **the most trusted online resource for finishing salt** — and convert that
> trust into jar reservations. This maps every search intent from beginner to expert, organised
> into 8 clusters, with the economics and build spec for each.

## Why this is a *moat* and not just a content plan

Three of these clusters — **Salt Harvesting**, **Mediterranean Traditions**, and **Ingredient
Quality** — cannot be written truthfully by a competitor selling mined, blended, or additive-
treated salt. That asymmetry is the moat. We lead with the things only a single-origin,
hand-raked Aegean producer can say, and let that earned trust flow into the commercial pages.

## The beginner → expert intent ladder

A reader matures through stages; the cluster set is built to meet them at each and walk them down
to a purchase.

```
BEGINNER        "what is fleur de sel?"  ·  "is sea salt healthy?"  ·  "how do I use it?"
   │            (Clusters 1, 2 — high traffic, low direct intent, top of funnel)
INTERMEDIATE    "fleur de sel vs Maldon"  ·  "best salt for steak"  ·  "salt for chocolate"
   │            (Clusters 3, 5 — high buyer intent, the money layer)
EXPERT          "how is fleur de sel harvested"  ·  "what salt do chefs finish with"  ·  terroir
                (Clusters 4, 6, 7, 8 — the trust moat; lower volume, highest credibility)
```

Legend — Traffic: 🔵 High (5k+) · 🟢 Med (1–5k) · 🟡 Low (<1k) *(directional US estimates — validate
before committing budget).* Buyer intent ★–★★★★★. Conversion potential ★–★★★★★ (likelihood a
visit assists/drives a reservation).
